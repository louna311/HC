Mercedes-Benz But Your Way!

For over 120 years, Mercedes‑Benz has been tailoring vehicles to individual desires. As the automobile has evolved, so has the art of customization. At MANUFAKTUR, you can choose from exclusive colors, elegant upholsteries, and refined trims – creating a car that reflects who you are.

Services

MANUFAKTUR allows you to customize your car in the shade that reflects your personality. From Night Black Magno to Ireland Mid Green Metallic, each hue is carefully selected, ensuring your star shines even brighter.

Pioneers stay pioneers

Mercedes‑Benz’s pioneering achievements in safety include ABS and ESP® technology, among many others. We were also the first car manufacturer to publicly conduct a frontal offset crash between two electric vehicles. In 2021.

Design in every detail

MANUFAKTUR enhances your vehicle's interior with exclusive upholsteries in multiple colors and designs. Some customers are so inspired by this craftsmanship that one even had a sofa made to match his MANUFAKTUR interior.

Full-body check for ideal transparency

In collaboration with the Fraunhofer Institute for High-Speed Dynamics – the EMI (Ernst Mach Institute) in Freiburg, Germany – Mercedes‑Benz recently became the world’s first car manufacturer to get a high-speed X-ray video taken from a crash test.

A personal assistant, always at your service

Digital innovations like AI learning transform our vehicles into the ultimate companions that don’t just understand your individual preferences and needs, but will anticipate your wishes to offer you a tailor-made experience for effortless comfort.
